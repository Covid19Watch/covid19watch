Incubation Period
How long till I show start showing signs ? 2-14 days represents the current official estimated range for the novel coronavirus COVID-19, But longer periods have been reported !
Mortality Rate
No prob, it's just like the flu ! Or is it ?! WHO Director-General Dr. Tedros Adhanom Ghebreyesus stated In his opening remarks at the March 3 media briefing on Covid-19 that 3.4% of the global reported Covid-19 cases have died whereas far fewer than 1% of those infected with seasonal flu die.
